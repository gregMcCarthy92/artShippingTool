| Step 6-1 |
| Basically Uppity Movers/Handypersons |



Yes, we are a pretentious people. We like to think that there is something special about us besides the fact they you pay us extra to be careful that makes it so we are unable to break valuable things. This, is not true. There is nothing that seperates us from movers, honestly, besides a BFA and a more robust insurance policy. However, this is good for you because it means that we are so scared of breaking your art — and possibly messing up your show — that we will probably go out of our way to ensure that your taxadermy horse head, or pile of dust from Mt. St Hellens makes it to the final desination in one piece. Or, at the very least, we'll be wearing nitrile gloves so we won't get finger prints on your art as we break it.
