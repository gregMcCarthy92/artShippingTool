| Step 2-1 |
| Get your Shit Together |



You are probably working out of shared studio. Thats fine, we all are if we are even that lucky. This means that you have no space for anything, and you won't any time soon. Lets try to not make this worse than it has to be and only have you pick up the things that you actually need. Your back catalouge of stuff that isn't sold is enough of astorage problem, lets not add to it. We aren't going to start packing yet, but it is good the have your stuff ready.

Just remember that although Uline seems like the best place to get stuff, the company is run by complete assholes. But fear not, there are plenty of place to get your things that won't give those losers your money. If you are in Toronto, you can stip by Superframe over on Geary Ave. They reuse all of the ethafoam and various wrapping materials and after a couple uses, they end up in a pile of bags that are free for artists to take. You can just walk in the door and ask for some, they are happy to give it to you. If you need more resouces, REFUSE ULINE has a whole section for canadian options.


This is how chat GPT imagines the process. We see the artist trying to put a painting that “doesn’t look that big” into a Dodge Caravan. These vehicles are widely believed to fit all objects made by humankind. We will be working over the next several steps to dispel these illusions.
