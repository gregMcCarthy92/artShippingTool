Subject: Inquiry Regarding Artwork Shipping from Toronto to Montreal

Hello [Gallery Name] Team,

I’m looking to follow up on shipping my artwork for (INSERT THE NAME OF YOUR SHOW/FAIR/CLIENT), and I just wanted to check on some details.

  1. What are the hours in which you would be able to receive the artwork, and where will anyone around be able to help receive the shipment?

  2. Do you have a loading dock?

  3. Do you have any preferred shippers you like to work with?

  4. Do you generally cover insuring the artwork during transport, or is that the artist's responsibility?

  5. Is there a timeline you would like me to follow? How far in advance do you need the artwork to be able to install it comfortably?

  6. Is there a contact person I should deal with directly?

Thanks for your assistance with this,

[Your Name]
[Your Address]
[Your Phone Number]
