You're
Done!


| Step 9-1 |
| Move on With Your Life |



Congratulations! You're Done! Now you can bask in the glory of a job well done. You have made a sale or got your stuff to a show. Who Cares! It's over!

There is only one catch, There is a good chance that you might have to take everything you just got to wherever it is that you got it, right back where it came from. Bummer right? Well, I can offer you on;y one piece of advice, Tell whoeever it is that has your stufff not to throw out the packaging until whatever you are doing is over or the money has changed hands. You've already spent enough on this and having to buy all that meterial all over again is not fun.

But why dwell on that! enjoy your life! have a beer, pat a dog, do whatever it is that you like to do. It is over!
