The
Followup


| Step 7-1 |
| More Emails — What Fun |



Now's the time. You're almost ready to start packing up your stuff, but wait! You need to check som ethings first! If there is going to be an issue with your work getting recieved, or if the reciever has som ekind of special requirements, you need to know that first. Well, there is a solution for that. Email. I know, it is boring sending emails, but you are better off knowing this now instead of having everything packed up like crazy expecting you'd have to fed ex everything, and then have the gallery call and ask when they should send the Art Handlers over to pack and transport your work. Yup, 5 minutes of typing is probably better than a whole day and $100 worth of supplies being wasted.



| You Can Just Copy and Paste This |




Subject: Inquiry Regarding Artwork Shipping from [ORIGIN] to [DESTINATION]

Hello [GALLERY NAME] Team,

I’m looking to follow up on shipping my artwork for [NAME OF YOUR SHOW/FAIR/CLIENT], and I just wanted to check on some details.

  1. What are the hours in which you would be able to receive the artwork, and where will anyone around be able to help receive the shipment?

  2. Do you have a loading dock?

  3. Do you have any preferred shippers you like to work with?

  4. Do you generally cover insuring the artwork during transport, or is that the artist's responsibility?

  5. Is there a timeline you would like me to follow? How far in advance do you need the artwork to be able to install it comfortably?

  6. Is there a contact person I should deal with directly?

Thanks for your assistance with this,

[Your Name]
[Your Address]
[Your Phone Number]

See, not so bad right?
